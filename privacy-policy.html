Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Altar ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Altar: Put God First mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials to manage your account and sync your data across devices.

Usage Data: We collect information about your app usage, including reading progress, streaks, and devotional history, to provide personalized features and track your spiritual journey.

Screen Time Data: Altar uses Apple's Family Controls and Screen Time APIs to manage app shielding on your device. This data is processed entirely on your device by Apple's frameworks and is never transmitted to our servers. We do not collect, store, or have access to your Screen Time data or the specific apps you choose to shield.

Camera: If you use the Scan to Unlock feature, your device's camera is used to scan Bible passages. Images are processed on-device and are not stored or transmitted.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's features
  • To sync your reading progress and preferences across devices
  • To manage your subscription and account
  • To improve the app experience

Data Storage and Security

Your account and usage data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and secure authentication.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Cloud database and authentication
  • Apple StoreKit: Subscription and purchase management
  • Apple Family Controls: On-device app shielding (no data leaves your device)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data for advertising or tracking.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen within the app. Upon account deletion, all your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Altar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

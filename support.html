Frequently Asked Questions

How does app shielding work?

Altar uses Apple's Screen Time and Family Controls APIs to shield distracting apps on your device. All Screen Time data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers. You choose which apps to shield, and Altar helps you stay focused on what matters.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open Altar, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > Altar, then tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I delete my account?

Open Altar, go to Settings, and tap "Delete Account." All your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

The app isn't blocking apps correctly

Make sure you've granted Altar the Screen Time permission in your device settings. Go to Settings > Screen Time > Altar and ensure access is enabled.
